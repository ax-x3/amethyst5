To my fellow queer people,

Despite the current situation regarding LGBTQIA+ rights in the US, there are things you can do.

If you reside in a high-risk state, your safety is your first priority. Stock up on medication and create an emergency plan. Keep an eye on any news that affects your region and alert others about risks.

Remember that as queer people, existing in spite of oppression is our strongest form of protest. You must live. You must fight. They cannot get rid of us because we are a naturally-occuring, inextinguishable, inseparable part of humanity.

Welcome!

My name is Amethyst. School keeps me pretty busy, but in my free time I like to make art, websites, and robots.

I also enjoy 3D printing, going places, and my bed. Despite that, I don't get enough sleep, and I spend too much time online.

This is the fourth time I've rebuilt my website, because every time I do, I get a newer, better idea.

Anyway, enjoy this disorganized, illegible, text‑wall‑of‑a‑website that for some reason I now prefer over minimalism. :3
