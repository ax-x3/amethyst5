About meh

I probably don't want to reveal too much PII since this is public.

But anyway, you already know my name. It's Amethyst. I'm a 16 year old living in the eastern United States just waiting for the weekend to arrive. I like to draw stuff, design stuff, and build stuff. Honestly, I just love creating things.

And, of course, I like to make websites. It's so cool to me that you can leave random stuff on the internet for strangers to come across.

About this site

This is the fourth iteration of my personal website. And as of its creation, it's also my favorite! It's inspired by several early-internet aesthetic sites I saw on Neocities. See the credits for more details.

Ever since I took a JavaScript & HTML class in school, I've enjoyed writing code from the ground up and watching a site grow. This is the culmination of all the skills I've collected over the span of one year.

I used to keep the older verions of my site running whenever I updated, but I found it to be a hassle and not worth the time. They're all publicly available on GitHub anyway.

I promise I'll put something over here someday.
