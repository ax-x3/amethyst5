Welcome!

While traversing the vast internet, you stumble across... me?

Welcome to my homepage! You can call me Amethyst or Aivi (eye-vee). This domain is my corner of cyberspace, and it's all about my weird self and my many, many interests.

To be clear, this site wasn't made for everyone! There's some swearing, NSFW outlinks, and the occasional rant into more serious topics. Also, this site is pretty graphics-heavy, so it looks best on a big screen powered by a decent computer running a popular browser.

If you like my site, the coolest thing you could do is leave a message in my guestbook. You'd be one of the first, and you'd absolutely make my day.


This site updates frequently! Please press control + shift + R.
