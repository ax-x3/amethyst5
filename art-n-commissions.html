Commissions

I do digital art commissions! Most of my work is furry/anthro art since that's what I enjoy drawing. If you don't know what that is, then I wouldn't reccomend commissioning me.

If you are interested, I'd certainly love to work with you! Keep in mind that I'm still somewhat new to this.

On the plus side, that means my prices are pretty low!

I've drawn 8 commissions so far.

Terms
of
Service





General

I reserve the right to refuse a commission request and/or terminate a commission that was previously approved.


Requests

There are limits to my abilities and to what I am willing to draw. Here are some (but not all) of the things that I will not accept:

  • Sexual content
  • Discriminatory content
  • Gore
  • Photorealism
  • Extremely complex pieces
  • Vague or no-ref prompts
  • Copyrighted work

My approval/denial of a commission is final for that specific commission.


Contact

You can reach out to me on Discord with any questions. Discord is my preferred method of contact, as it is easy for me to find your references and the description of your commission.

Please make your commission request as clear as possible to avoid any misunderstanding. Only commission me if you can make your requests in English. You can use a translation tool, but beware of mistranslations.

I will try my best to keep you updated throughout the commissioning process. Do understand, however, that unforeseeable circumstances can emerge and cause delay or cancellation of a commission.


Payment

Payments will be made through PayPal. For particularly large commissions, or if I haven't gotten to know you prior to the commission, one-half of the fee must be paid upfront. The other half will be paid after you recieve the final artwork.

We can discuss payment as long as the commission is less than halfway complete.


Usage

I reserve the right to display commission artwork in my portfolio(s), i.e. DeviantArt, my website, and other social media.

You can post commissioned art as long as it is not used promotionally and proper credit is given.

COMMISSIONED ARTWORK IS FOR PERSONAL USE ONLY. COMMERCIAL USE OF ANY KIND IS NOT ALLOWED.

DO NOT RESELL MY ARTWORK OR CLAIM THAT YOU CREATED IT.

Getting Started

Done reading? Shoot me a DM request on Discord.

Please make your intentions clear in the first DM, otherwise you may be filtered out as spam.

Follow the No Hello principle.



If I'm not responsive, try tagging me on Bluesky.

If that doesn't work, then try sending me an email.
