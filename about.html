My name is Amethyst. I'm a 16 year old INFP-T from the United States, always waiting for the weekend to come around. I like to draw stuff, design stuff, and build stuff. I love rough sketches, Helvetica Neue, and cardboard boxes.

My adoration for creating all sorts of things has been a part of me for as long as I remember. My various projects bring joy into my life, but in the end, I hope to leave the world with more value than it had when I found it.
