Me



Aivi, my fursona!

I'm Amethyst (INFP-T), a 16 year old from the United States, just waiting for the weekend to arrive. I like to draw stuff, design stuff, and build stuff. Honestly, I just love to create things.

I've had a love for creating for as long as I've been alive. I'm driven by that love, and through it, I hope to leave the world with more than it had when I got here.

Life is pretty fuckin' weird, and I still have to deal with it for who knows how many more years. Eventually, something will appear out of nowhere and bring me out of this existence. I haven't got a clue when or how, but that doesn't matter. If I only consider the length of life, then I won't experience all the momentary joy. If I only think about the future, I'll get lost in the present. Because of that, I like to work on many little things that I can be proud of. Of course, I'm only human, so I'm no stranger to stupid choices and wasting time. But to quote one of my most admirable past teachers, it only matters that "you be here now."

Buuuut whatever. If you're reading this, I love you. Thanks for taking the time to be here. I write this stuff specifically for you to read, and since you're reading it, it's achieved its purpose.

Also, if you want to let me know you were here, you should sign the Guestbook!
